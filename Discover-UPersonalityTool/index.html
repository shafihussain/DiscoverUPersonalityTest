Personality Test - A, B, C, D

The 4 letters personality types is based off of the proto-psychological theory that suggests that there are four fundamental personality types:

  • The "A" personality type consists (impatient, controlling, energetic, highly competitive, ambitious, and have a difficult time relaxing).
  • The "B" personality type consists (relaxed, patient and easy-going).
  • The "C" personality type is known as someone who is (introverted, respectful, easger to please and compliant).
  • The "D" personality type experience levels of (anxiety, irritation, and depressed mood).
